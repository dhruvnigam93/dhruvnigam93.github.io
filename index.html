Dhruv Nigam

ML Engineer & Researcher

I enjoy working at the intersection of math, coding, and business. I aim to build systems, not models. Models are the results of well-designed training systems. Right now, my ambition is to build systems that create the next generation of AI models.

Currently, I'm debugging voice pipelines and optimizing throughput and latency to reduce conversational latency to 1.2ms. I'm experimenting with deployment regions, protocols (TCP vs. UDP!), and model inference engines.

Previously, I trained my own foundational models to predict user behavior trajectories using a transformer backbone with many modifications to the standard LLM architecture. Validated my hypothesis that scaling laws emerge across different domains, not just text.

Even earlier, I worked in finance, where there was no room for error, no patience for latency, and millions on the line. That experience taught me the value of rigor in engineering and modeling.

Interested in post-training, inference, agentic systems, and scaling ML.
