Dhruv Nigam

ML Engineer & Researcher

I enjoy working at the intersection of math, software, and business. I build systems, not models. Models are the outcomes of well-designed training and feedback systems. Right now, my ambition is to build systems that create the next generation of AI models.

Lately my team and I launched the first live sports AI influencer. I'm debugging voice pipelines and optimizing conversational latency. I'm toying with communication protocols (TCP vs. UDP), model inference engines and reinforcement learning to make models feel more human.

Previously, I trained my own 200M foundational model to predict user behavior trajectories using a transformer backbone with slight modifications to the standard LLM architecture. Increased DAU by 3% overnight. Validated my hypothesis that scaling laws emerge across different domains, not just text.

Even earlier, I worked as a quant and had millions (not mine) ride on my trading algorithms. There was no room for error, no patience for latency, and real money on the line. That experience taught me the value of rigor in engineering and modeling.

Interested in LLM post-training, inference, and agentic systems.
